ALL ABOUT SOUL FOUNDATION.



There are nearly hundreds and thousands of children who suffers from various challenges due to poverty, war, immigration and poor healthcare. Therefore our foundation takes the initiative to enhance their quality of living by your doantions and charity.

Running a foundation for children affected by war, poverty, and other hardships is a profound commitment to making a positive impact. In the face of adversity, the foundation strives to provide crucial humanitarian aid, education, and counseling services.

By addressing their immediate needs and fostering long-term empowerment, the foundation aims to create resilient and hopeful futures for these deserving young individuals, breaking the cycle of poverty and offering a path toward healing and opportunity.
